amiport

POSIX tools ported to AmigaOS 3.x by AI-assisted pipeline.
Each tool is cross-compiled for 68000+ and tested on real AmigaOS.

Install: Download the .lha file, transfer to your Amiga, extract with:
lha x filename.lha SYS:
